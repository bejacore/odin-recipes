Banana Protein Shake

Description

This is a sweet protein shake that contains a high natural protein content and it can be consumed before or after a workout.

Ingredients

  • Milk.
  • Soy milk.
  • Oats.
  • Two bananas.
  • Chia.
  • Peanut butter.
  • Cinnamon.
  • Ice.

Steps

In a blender add:

  1. 1/4 liters of milk.
  2. 33 grams of soy milk.
  3. 27 grams of oats.
  4. 15 gram of chia.
  5. One tablespoon of peanut butter.
  6. Two bananas in slices.
  7. Cinnamon to taste.
  8. Some ice.
  9. Blend for thirty seconds.
